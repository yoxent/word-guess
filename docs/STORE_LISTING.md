## Full description

```
Word Guess is a free word puzzle game for Android. Crack today’s words with everyone else, or play at your own pace in Endless and Random modes.

HOW TO PLAY
• Guess the secret word one letter at a time
• Green = right letter, right spot
• Yellow = right letter, wrong spot
• Gray = not in the word
• Words can be 5–10 letters long — pick your challenge
• Hard Mode forces you to use the clues you’ve already found

GAME MODES
• Daily Challenge — up to six puzzles a day (one per length 5–10), the same words for all players (UTC)
• Endless — keep the streak going across back-to-back rounds
• Random — a surprise length every game

PROGRESS & COMPETITION
• Track wins, streaks, guess distribution, and results by word length
• Optional Google Play Games sign-in to sync stats and climb the boards
• Leaderboards: Daily streak, Endless streak, Endless words, Best streak, and Sharpshooter
• Resume unfinished Endless and Random games when you come back
• See the word’s definition after each game
• Share your result grid with friends

HELP WHEN YOU NEED IT
• Watch a short ad for an extra attempt or a letter hint
• Or go Pro with a one-time upgrade: no interstitial ads, plus one extra rewarded-attempt allowance (hints still use rewarded ads)

LOOK & FEEL
• Light, dark, or system theme
• Background music and sound effects with separate volume controls
• Optional haptic feedback
• Simpler Animations if you prefer less motion

Privacy policy: https://yoxent.github.io/word-guess/privacy

Word Guess by Vorith Studio.
```
