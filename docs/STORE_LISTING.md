## Full description

```
Word Guess is a free word puzzle game for Android. Crack today’s word with everyone else, or play at your own pace in Free, Random, and Endless modes.

HOW TO PLAY
• Guess the secret word one letter at a time
• Green = right letter, right spot
• Yellow = right letter, wrong spot
• Gray = not in the word
• Words can be 5–10 letters long — pick your challenge

GAME MODES
• Daily Challenge — the same word for all players each day (UTC)
• Free Play — choose the word length and go
• Random — a surprise length every game
• Endless — keep the streak going across back-to-back rounds
• Hard Mode — a global toggle that forces you to use what you’ve already learned

PROGRESS & COMPETITION
• Track wins, streaks, and guess distribution
• Optional Google Play Games sign-in to sync stats and climb Daily & Endless leaderboards
• Resume unfinished games when you come back

HELP WHEN YOU NEED IT
• Watch a short ad for an extra attempt or a letter hint
• Or go Pro with a one-time upgrade to remove interstitial ads (rewards for hints still available)

MADE TO FEEL GOOD
• Light and dark themes
• Sound and haptics
• Accessibility-friendly options

Privacy policy: https://yoxent.github.io/word-guess/privacy

Word Guess by Vorith Studio.
```
